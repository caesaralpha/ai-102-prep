## Tips for Using Video Content Effectively

1. **Don't just watch passively** — pause and try the demos yourself in your own Azure subscription
2. **Take notes** — use the cheatsheet format from [09-cheatsheet.md](09-cheatsheet.md)
3. **Watch at 1.25x–1.5x speed** for review sessions, normal speed for first watch
4. **Focus on NLP section** — it's 30-35% of the exam (the biggest chunk)
5. **Always cross-reference** with [official Microsoft documentation](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/ai-services/) — videos may not cover every detail
6. **Take the official practice assessment** before and after watching — track your improvement
7. **Don't skip hands-on labs** — the exam tests practical knowledge, not just theory
